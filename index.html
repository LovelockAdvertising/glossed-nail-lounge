Good to know

Your questions, answered

The little details before you book, so you turn up knowing exactly what to expect. ✨

What exactly is BIAB?

BIAB stands for Builder In A Bottle: a soft builder gel applied only to your natural nails. It's lighter and kinder than acrylic, protects your own nails as they grow, and gives that glossy, durable finish every Glossed set is known for.

How long will my nails last?

With proper aftercare, your BIAB nails can last 3-4 weeks before needing an infill. Everyone's nails grow at different rates, so the ideal time between appointments can vary depending on your nail growth and lifestyle.

To help your nails last as long as possible, avoid using them as tools, wear gloves when cleaning, and keep your cuticles hydrated with cuticle oil.

Should I book an infill or a new set?

You don't need to worry about choosing. I offer one standard BIAB appointment, and we'll decide together whether your nails are best suited for an infill or a fresh set when you arrive.

Whether your nails simply need an infill, a few have broken and need replacing, or we decide a completely fresh set is the best option, it's all included in the same service and priced the same. This means you don't have to guess what to book, I'll choose the best option for your nails on the day to ensure you leave with the best possible result.

How do I choose my tier?

Pick the tier that matches how much detail you want, from clean and simple (Essential) all the way to fully custom art (Elite). Still deciding? Send me an inspo pic and I'll point you to the right one.

What if I don't know what design I want?

That's completely okay. We'll go through ideas together during your appointment and brainstorm a design that suits your style, lifestyle, and nail goals.

I'm always happy to help you figure out something you'll love, so you'll never leave without the perfect set.

How long will my appointment take?

Appointment times vary depending on your nail goals and design, but most sets take around 1.5 to 2.5 hours. More detailed nail art may take longer, and I always allow enough time so your nails aren't rushed.

What forms of payment do you accept?

I accept both cash and card payments for your convenience.

Do you do removals?

Yes, I offer both BIAB removals for my own work and removals of previous work from other nail technicians.

Please make sure to add a removal to your booking when scheduling your appointment so I can allow enough time to safely and properly remove the product before your new set.

Do you require a deposit?

No, I don't require a deposit to secure your appointment. However, I do keep a card on file when you book in case of no-shows or late cancellations.

If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, I kindly ask that you provide as much notice as possible so I can offer your appointment time to another client. Details regarding any cancellation or rescheduling fees can be found in my Cancellation Policy.

How do I keep my nails looking fresh?

Pop cuticle oil on daily, wear gloves for cleaning and dishes, and never use your nails as tools. Treat them like the tiny artworks they are, book your infill in time, and they'll stay glossy.

Where are you located?

I'm based from a private home salon in Robina, Gold Coast. The salon address is sent to you in your reminder text 24 hours before your appointment, so you'll have everything you need before your appointment.

Can I bring a friend or my children to my appointment?

To keep the salon a comfortable and relaxing space for everyone, I kindly ask that you attend your appointment on your own. As Glossed Nail Lounge is a private home salon, there isn't enough space to accommodate additional guests or children.
